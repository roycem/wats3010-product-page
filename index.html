Bumby.

A better, cheaper, easier, lazier, more sustainable way to approach farm fresh produce.

Bumby. is more than a bi-weekly produce subscription box. It’s your trip to the grocery store. Your one extra hour of me time. Your step in the right direction of being more sustainable. It’s a better, cheaper, easier, lazier, eco-friendly approach to farm fresh produce. A Bumby Box membership includes two weeks worth of regional and seasonal vegetables and fruits, all packaged in a compostable compost bin. Every other week, you’ll get a box of goodies sent straight from a different local farm, so it’s the next best thing to picking it yourself. Once you get your box, all you have to do is store the goods and then put the food waste back in your Bumby Box. It’s lock-in top and double layered bottom mean no seepage and no stink.Once you get your next box in the mail, all you have to do is compost it in your building’s compost bin - or you can leave it out for our delivery people and we’ll take care of it for you. It’s really that easy.
